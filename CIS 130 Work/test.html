Candy, Dinner, and Drinks

The most popular category of gifts according to most surveys. Wine is a popular enough gift, but sits as the least popular gift to give out of the three at least as a solitary item though when included as part of a romantic dinner it becomes substantially more popular. Speaking of which, romantic dinners are quite popular sitting at number four and being only slightly behind Greeting Cards and Flowers as a gift. Finally though we come to the real number one, candy and other sweets. They dominate the field being the single most popular category in the US, and have a commanding lead in most surveys.
